Site Name

Site Name: Hispanic Help Center (HHC)

The name Hispanic Help Center was chosen because the website will be focused on providing helpful information and services to the Hispanic community. The acronym HHC is easy to remember and represents a place where people can find guidance, support, and services.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Hispanic Help Center (HHC) website is to provide information and services to the Hispanic community in areas such as tax preparation, immigration assistance, and community support. The website will allow visitors to learn about available services, contact the center, and find helpful resources in Spanish.

Scenarios

  • What services does the Hispanic Help Center provide for the community?
  • How can I contact the Hispanic Help Center to get help with taxes or immigration paperwork?
